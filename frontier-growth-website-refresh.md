## FAQ

### Are homeowner campaigns the same as realtor campaigns?

No. Realtor campaigns focus on referral relationships with agents. Homeowner campaigns focus on direct outreach to property owners and homeowners.

### Can campaigns be limited by geography?

Yes. Campaigns can be focused by city, ZIP code, neighborhood, or service area.

### Can we run realtor and homeowner campaigns together?

Yes. Many businesses benefit from both: realtor campaigns build referral relationships, while homeowner campaigns create direct market visibility.

### Is availability limited?

Yes. Market and category availability may be limited depending on your service and location.

# Design Direction

## Overall Feel

The refreshed site should feel like a focused B2B growth platform for local service businesses: clear, practical, confident, and modern.

Avoid generic startup language and overly abstract visuals. The site should show real-world context: homes, neighborhoods, agents, inboxes, maps, and campaign activity.

## Visual Priorities

- Strong first-screen headline
- Clear split between Realtor Campaigns and Homeowner Campaigns
- Simple process visuals
- Local-market exclusivity callouts
- Direct CTAs throughout the page
- Testimonials and proof near the middle and bottom of pages
